The Faculty of

Technology

Engineering sciences was officially created within the Saad DAHLAB university of Blida 1 by executive decree n°98-253 of 24 Rabi Ethani 1419 corresponding to August 17, 1998 modifying and supplementing decree n°83- 544 of September 24, 1983 on the standard statute of the university. It became Faculty of Technology in 2012

Dean's word

Welcome to the website of the Faculty of Technology of Blida1 University.
A country such as Algeria must strive to acquire the means of technological development, particularly in this era of globalization. Spearhead of the partnership of our University with its environment, the Faculty of Technology plans to play the leading roles in this path traced by the management. With a significant human and material potential of lecturers, administrative and support staff as well as several research laboratories, the faculty is always ready for its mission of transferring knowledge to future generations. A staff of more than 9000 students is supervised and trained in master's degree and doctorate. The courses offered cover the Science and Technology Domain in 8 key departments ranging from preparatory classes for Grandes Ecoles to renewable energies. The training programs are made according to the norms and standards to meet the expectations of the local, regional, national and global socio-economic environment. Our immediate ambition is to act towards a better openness of the faculty to the industrial world, a label of quality training. This is achieved through the organization of meetings, the weaving of preferential relations and the establishment of cooperation agreements. The scientific and educational fruits will be used for wider production and dissemination of knowledge, animation and initiation of the debate of ideas and search for solutions, as well as the contribution to the international influence and refining of the talents of tomorrow.

Deans biography

Ali Aouabed Dean of the Faculty of Technology State engineer Born in Tazmalt (Bejaia) in 1957, Ali Aouabed did his primary studies in four schools (Boufarik, Gouraya, El affroun and Blida) and his secondary studies at Ibn Rouch high school in Blida. The University of Bab Ezzouar (USTHB) welcomes him for the two years of common core. He obtained his state engineer diploma in sanitary engineering from the National Polytechnic School in 1984. He taught water treatment from 1984 to 1990 as an assistant at USTHB Mutations in Blida and Magister In 1990, he requested his transfer to the University of Blida where he joined the environmental engineering department of the Institute of Industrial Chemistry. In 1991, he obtained his master's degree in environmental engineering at the USTHB Head of department-PhD student From 1991 to 1998, he held the position of head of the environmental engineering department. In 1998, he began his state doctorate on the treatment of water from the Keddara dam. This work allowed him to stay for 15 months at the Laboratory of Environmental Process Engineering (LIPE) at INSA Toulouse. University professor In March 2003, he supported his State Doctorate in Environmental Engineering at the University of Blida and became a University Professor in 2008. Vice Dean – Dean In addition to devoting himself to research and teaching, he held the positions of Vice Dean of the Faculty of Engineering Sciences (2003-2006) and Dean of this faculty from 2006 to 2011 Research and UNESCO Its research program focuses on water treatment through the use of new bioflocculants as well as on the management and treatment of urban solid waste.
